Network Maintenance & Administration

As your IT partner Kenlar IT delivers professional and total responsibility for all facets of your network management and network administration. Combined with our remote IT support services Kenlar IT can provide for 100% of your network management and administration needs enabling your organization to fully utilize all the capabilities of your network and IT infrastructure.


No matter what size network you have, Kenlar IT can manage the day to day management and administration of the network including:

  • User and group administration
  • Permission Management
  • Group membership
  • Security policy management
  • Group policy administration
  • Internet access
  • E-mail distribution lists
  • Remote access permissions
  • System log analysis and administration
  • Performance monitoring
  • Network monitoring

As part of our network management and administration services, Kenlar IT can also put systems in place to monitor all facets of your network operations. These systems can be configured to alert us or anyone you choose in a variety of methods such as e-mail, pagers and cell phones when almost any condition that can happen to a network, server or workstation occurs.

Network management and monitoring systems are of prime importance as integral components in a complete management, administration and network security platform by providing Intrusion Detection functionality and alerting the appropriate parties.
