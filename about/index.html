About Kenlar IT

The Kenlar IT team pride ourselves in delivering an exceptional service which surpasses expectation. With over 20 years experience in computer sales, PC repair and technical support, rest assured Kenlar IT staff are pioneers in today's digital age.

All team members are fully qualified technicians with Microsoft qualifications and educated to Degree level. Kenlar IT provides consumers and small businesses with a professional technical support service. With a 24 hour response service and fully qualified staff, the service is intended to minimise cost and maximise productivity.
